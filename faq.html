You can donate through our mobile number: 0744612705 or bank account: 60975433567886654. We accept both one-time and recurring donations. All donations are used to support our children's programs and facilities.
What programs do you offer for children?
We offer various programs including spiritual guidance, educational support, personal development workshops, and community engagement activities. Our programs are designed to nurture both the spiritual and personal growth of children.
How can I volunteer with your ministry?
We welcome volunteers who share our vision. You can apply through our website or contact us directly. We have opportunities in teaching, mentoring, administrative support, and event organization.
Where are you located?
Our ministry is located in Kasese, Uganda. We serve the local community and surrounding areas, providing support to children and families in need.
How are donations used?
All donations are used directly for our children's programs, including educational materials, meals, facility maintenance, and program development. We maintain full transparency in our financial operations.
