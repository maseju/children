About Our Ministry

"God Is Our Saviour" - Serving Children in Kasese, Uganda

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing spiritual guidance, education, and support to children in need throughout Kasese, Uganda. Through God's grace and the generous support of our community, we strive to create a nurturing environment where every child can grow, learn, and develop their full potential.

Our Core Values

Faith

Keeping God at the center of all our work and decisions.

Compassion

Showing Christ's love through our care for children and families.

Service

Dedicated to serving our community with love and commitment.
